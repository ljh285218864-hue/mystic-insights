What we can really look at

Your Chinese destiny chart is like a map of your timing, patterns and nature. We use it to answer very specific questions, for example:

  • Love & marriage – “Should I stay in this relationship?”, “Is there real marriage energy for me in the next few years?”
  • Career – “Is this job or business worth the sacrifice?”, “What type of work fits my chart?”
  • Money – “Why does money come and go?”, “Is this a good timing for investment or expansion?”
  • Health – long-term patterns in energy, stress, weak areas in the body that need care.
  • Moving & relocation – “Is this move good for me?”, “Does this new city support my chart?”
  • Timing – which years/months are supportive, which are heavy, so you don’t keep pushing at the wrong time.

This is not fortune cookies.

I will not tell you “everything will be perfect” or “you’re cursed forever”. You get a direct, honest answer based on your chart – with clear explanation in normal language.

You can ask about relationships, work, money, family, children, life direction or a decision you are stuck on. We stay grounded and practical, not superstitious.

Readings are private. Your birth data and questions are only used to prepare your reading and are not shared with anyone.

Common questions

Is this reading scary or fatalistic?

No. We talk honestly about strengths and weak spots, and about easier and harder timing, but the goal is to help you make better decisions – not to scare you or tell you everything is fixed forever.

What if I don’t know my exact birth time?

If you don’t know your time at all, we can still work with a limited view (mainly big patterns and timing). If you know roughly morning/afternoon/evening, say that in the form and I’ll adjust.

Can I ask about someone else?

You can ask about a relationship dynamic if you have their birth data, but the focus stays on what you can do and how your own chart is involved. We don’t spy on other people’s lives.
