Common questions

A few short answers so clients know what to expect before they book.

Do you tell me exact events?

No one can guarantee exact events. Instead, we focus on your patterns, timing, and likely themes so you can make better decisions with the energy you have.

What if I don’t know my exact birth time?

If you don’t know your birth time, we can still work, but some details may be more general. Please share as much as you know (for example “around 8–9am”).

Can I record the session?

Yes. You can record the call for your own personal use so you don’t have to remember everything at once.
