Frequently asked questions

What information do you need from me?

Your full name, date of birth, time of birth (if known), city of birth, your current city, time zone, and your main questions or focus areas.

What if I don’t know my exact birth time?

If you don’t know your birth time, we can still work with a more general chart. Some details may be more broad, but we can still see patterns and timing.

Can you tell me exactly what will happen?

No one can guarantee exact events. This reading focuses on your natural tendencies and timing so you can make better decisions with your own free will.
