Shop FAQ

Because this is a small-batch spiritual shop, returns are limited. If an item arrives damaged, we’ll work with you to replace or refund it. Please contact us within 7 days of delivery with photos.

Do I need a reading or feng shui session to buy?

No — you can buy items on their own. However, many clients choose items after a destiny reading or feng shui consultation, so that each object has a clear role in their life or space.
