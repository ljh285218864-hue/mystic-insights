About the practitioner

I work with traditional Chinese destiny reading and Feng Shui, with over 30 years around these systems.

My style is direct but kind. No “you are cursed forever”, no fake promises that everything will be perfect. I tell you what your chart and your space are really showing right now, and what can be adjusted.

I mainly work with clients in the U.S. and Europe who feel stuck in love, work or health and want a clear, grounded way to move forward – not more guessing.

  • Clear, honest language – no jargon, no doom.
