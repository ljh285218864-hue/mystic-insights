About this work

Chinese destiny & Feng Shui, done in a clear and grounded way.

I’ve worked with Chinese destiny reading and Feng Shui for more than 30 years. I grew up with this system, and over time I learned how to translate it into language that busy, modern people can actually use.

My clients are mostly in the U.S., but the questions are the same everywhere: “Will this relationship last?” “Should I stay at this job?” “Why does my home feel so heavy?”

I don’t do dramatic scare tactics or tell you that you are “cursed” unless you buy something. I read your chart and space as honestly as I can, then give you practical next steps so you can feel calmer and more in control.

All readings and consults are written replies in English, usually by WhatsApp. You can take your time to read, save the messages, and come back to them when you need.

What you can expect

  • 30+ years of practice

    Long-term work with Chinese destiny and Feng Shui, blended with a realistic view of modern life.

  • Direct, honest answers

    If a time is challenging, I will say so. If it’s supportive, I’ll show you how to use it. No vague “maybe” talk.

  • Respect for your beliefs

    You can be spiritual, skeptical, or somewhere in between. My job is not to convert you, only to read clearly.

  • Clear boundaries

    I don’t replace doctors, therapists or lawyers. If you are in crisis, I will always advise you to seek local professional help first.

How sessions work: you choose a package, pay on the website, then send your details through a WhatsApp button. I sit with your chart or photos and send you written replies within the stated time.

If you’re not sure which service fits you, you can always send a short message first and mention “I’m not sure which option to choose”.
