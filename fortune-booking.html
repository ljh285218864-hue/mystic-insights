Destiny reading – common questions

Is this a live call or written only?

All readings are written replies in English, usually by WhatsApp. There is no live phone or video call, so you can read everything in your own time and re-read later.

What kind of questions do you not answer?

I don’t predict exact death dates, diagnose illness, or give legal or lottery predictions. I also don’t replace medical, psychological, legal or financial professionals.

How long does it take to receive my reading?

Most readings are delivered within 24–48 hours after payment and sending your full details on WhatsApp, excluding weekends and public holidays. If there is any expected delay, I will let you know by message.

What if I don’t know my exact birth time?

Exact birth time is very helpful, but many people don’t know it. If you only know a rough time of day, you can still share that and we will work with what is available. If the time is completely unknown, I will focus more on patterns and timing that are less sensitive to the exact minute.

Can I get a refund if I change my mind?

Because this is a custom, one-on-one service, refunds are generally not offered once I have started working on your chart. If you need to change or cancel, please message me on WhatsApp as soon as possible after payment, and we will see what is possible in your situation.

Will you tell me only “good things” or also difficult timing?

I try to be honest and grounded. If a season is challenging, I will say so and give suggestions on how to move through it more safely. If the timing is supportive, I will show you how to use that window.
