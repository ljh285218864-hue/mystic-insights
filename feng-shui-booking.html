Feng Shui consult – common questions

Do you need to visit my home in person?

No. All consults are done online. You send clear photos, a short video and a simple sketch of your layout, and I work from there. Many clients are outside my city or country.

Will I need to rebuild my house or buy a lot of objects?

No. I focus on realistic changes: repositioning furniture, removing or moving certain items, and adding a few key pieces only when needed. You don’t need to start over or spend thousands.

How long does it take to receive my Feng Shui plan?

Most home plans are delivered within 2–4 days after payment and after receiving your photos, video and layout on WhatsApp, excluding weekends and public holidays.

Is this safe if I am renting and cannot change much?

Yes. Many clients rent. We focus on what you can change: bed position, desk position, clutter, symbolic objects and how you use different parts of the home.

Will you keep my photos and information private?

I use your photos, video and layout only for your consult. I don’t post your space online or share your details without permission.

Can you guarantee that my marriage, money or health will change?

No one can guarantee exact outcomes. Feng Shui can support you by removing pressure and improving flow in your environment, but it works together with your own actions, timing and life circumstances.
