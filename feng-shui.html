Frequently asked questions

A few common questions from people who are curious about Feng Shui but don’t want anything extreme or superstitious.

Do I need to “believe” in Feng Shui for it to work?

You don’t have to force yourself to believe anything. Many clients treat it as a mix of traditional wisdom and environmental psychology. We make changes, you live with them, and you notice how your sleep, mood, and focus respond.

Will you talk about curses or bad luck?

No. I don’t use fear-based language or talk about “curses”. We focus on what can be adjusted – layout, usage of rooms, and how your space supports your nervous system, relationships, and work.

Will you ask me to buy lots of products?

No upsell crystals. Classical Feng Shui starts with structure and positioning. Sometimes a few items help, but most suggestions use what you already own. My shop is optional, not required.

Can you work with apartments or rentals?

Yes. Many clients live in apartments or rentals with rules. I design changes that respect your limitations: furniture placement, what you see when you enter, and how you use each room.
