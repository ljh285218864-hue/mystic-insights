Frequently asked questions

Can feng shui be done online?

Yes. With a floor plan, photos, and video, we can see the main flows and blocks in your space. Many clients are outside my city, so sessions are designed for online work.

Do I need to buy a lot of new things?

Not necessarily. We first work with layout, placement, and what you already have. Any objects (like plants or specific feng shui items) are added only if truly helpful and realistic.

What if I rent or share my space?

We take your limitations seriously. Many clients live in rentals or share with family or roommates. We focus on changes you can control: your room, your desk, and how you use the space.
