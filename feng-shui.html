Common questions

Do I have to buy a lot of items?

No. We mainly work with layout, doors, beds, desks, light and clutter. If I suggest any items (like a lamp or plant), they are simple and easy to find. You don’t have to buy from my shop.

Is this the same as decorating?

Decoration is about how things look. Feng Shui is about how the space behaves – where you sleep, where you work, what you see when you walk in, and how your body feels there. Of course, it can look better too.

Will my life magically be perfect?

No. This is not magic. But living in a home that constantly pulls you down makes change much harder. My job is to stop your space from fighting you and start letting it support the work you’re already doing.
