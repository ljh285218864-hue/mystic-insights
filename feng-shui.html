My job is to take those traditional principles and turn them into clear steps you can actually do: move this, use this room differently, change this angle, add or remove this one thing.

Frequently asked questions

For people who are curious about Feng Shui but don’t want drama, superstition, or fear-based language.

Do I need to “believe” in Feng Shui for it to work?

No. You just need to be honest about what you want to change, and willing to experiment with your space. Many clients treat Feng Shui as a mix of ancient wisdom and environmental psychology.

Will you talk about curses or bad luck?

No. I don’t do fear-based readings. We focus on patterns, timing, and environment – what can actually be adjusted.

Can you work with apartments or rentals?

Yes. Many clients live in apartments or rentals with strict rules. We focus on layout, usage of rooms, and what you see every day, not knocking down walls.
