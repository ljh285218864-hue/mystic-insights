Questions you might be too polite to ask

Let’s answer the “Is this real?” and “Am I crazy for trying this?” questions directly.

“Do I have to believe in Feng Shui for it to work?”

No. You don’t have to force yourself to “believe” anything. You just need to be open enough to try changing your environment and see how your body, mood and life respond. We are combining traditional Chinese ideas with very practical changes.

“Is this about curses, ghosts, or scaring me?”

No. I don’t use fear language. No talk of “you are cursed” or “your house is bad luck”. We focus on patterns, directions, timing, and how your rooms are set up. The goal is to give you more support, not more fear.

“Will you tell me to throw away everything and buy all new stuff?”

No. We use what you have as much as possible. Sometimes one or two key items help, but the big shifts usually come from how you place things, how you use rooms, and what you see every day when you move through your home.

“Can you work with a small apartment or rental?”

Yes. Most people don’t live in huge houses. We work with apartments, shared spaces, rentals with rules, and all the real-life limitations you actually have.
